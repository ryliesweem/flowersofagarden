What does culture mean to you?

Take time to reflect on culture and write a response below. Use the questions below as a starting point for reflection.

  • How do you define culture?
  • Do you identify with a specific culture? If not what is your culture?
  • Do you have a group of people who also identify with the same culture? Do you feel close with these people?
  • Are you mostly friends with or work with people who identify with your culture?
  • Has having friends or working with people from a different culture expanded your worldview and caused you to grow?
  • Have you ever felt that because someone is from a different culture you cant become close to them?
  • Have you found yourself hiding your differences in order to blend into a particular culture?
